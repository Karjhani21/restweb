Valli's Secret Thali -A Culinary Adventure

A surprise, rotating thali (platter) experience where customers don't know the exact menu until it's served.
Every week (or even daily), a new themed thali is curated by the chef — inspired by a region, an ingredient, or even a mood.
Each thali could come with a handwritten card describing the story or inspiration behind it — evoking nostalgia, discovery, and tradition.
Let guests guess the region or ingredients used in the thali.
Winners get a free dessert or discount on their next visit.
